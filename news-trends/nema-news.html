        The National Electrical Manufacturers Association (NEMA) published ANSI/NEMA IM 60000-2021 Industrial Laminated Thermosetting Products by 2022. This new edition covers the manufacture, testing, and performance of laminated thermosetting products in the form of sheets, rods, and tubes.

        New Motors and Generators Guide Provides Accurate Motor Efficiency Predictions | 2/4/2022

        The National Electrical Manufacturers Association (NEMA) is pleased to announce the publication of NEMA MG G2-2021 A NEMA Motor and Generator (IS-MG) Section Guide for Validating an Alternative Efficiency Determination Method (AEDM). This new guide defines the process to validate a digital model for predicting the efficiency of electrical motors.
