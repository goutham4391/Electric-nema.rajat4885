        Directions

        Location

        • The National Electrical Manufacturers Association (NEMA) is located at:
          1300 North 17th Street, Suite 900
          Arlington, Virginia 22209
          USA

        View Larger Map

        • Get point-to-point driving directions.
        Directions

        From Washington, D.C.

        From the Rosslyn Metro station
        [visit Metro website]

        From Virginia (I-66)

        From Reagan National Airport (DCA)
        [visit DCA website]

        From Virginia (Rte. 50)

        From Dulles International Airport (IAD)
        [visit IAD website]

        From Virginia (I-395)

        From Baltimore Washington International Airport (BWI)
        [visit BWI website]

        From Washington, D.C.
        • Take Route 50 West (Constitution Avenue) to the Rosslyn exit.
        • At the top of the ramp, bear right onto N. Lynn going toward Rosslyn.
        • At the corner of N. Lynn and Wilson, go left onto Wilson.
        • At the corner of Wilson and N. Nash, turn left onto N. Nash.
        • Follow N. Nash for a block (it turns into N. 17th Street). Building is straight ahead.
        • Parking: Bear left; turn right into parking garage.

        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.

        From the Metro

        • Take the Orange/Blue/Silver line to the Rosslyn Metro stop.
        • Leave station through the Ft. Myer Drive exit.
        • Go left on Ft. Myer Dr. (corner of Wilson Blvd).
        • Go right on Wilson and walk two blocks up toward the Hyatt.
        • At the corner of N. Nash and Wilson Blvd., take left on N. Nash for a block (it turns into N. 17th).
        • Building is straight ahead.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.

        From Virginia via I- 66

        • Take 66 East toward Washington, D.C.
        • Take the Rosslyn/Key Bridge Exit.
        • At the second light (N. Ft. Myer) turn right (WestPark Hotel).
        • At first stop light turn right (this will turn into N. Nash Street).
        • Follow N. Nash Street over Wilson Boulevard (1 stop light).
        • N. Nash Street will dead-end at N. 17th Street. Building is straight ahead.
        • Parking: Bear left; turn right into parking garage.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.

        PLEASE NOTE: Route 66 East is HOV-2 from 5:30 to 9:30 a.m. Route 50 is an alternate route.

        From Reagan National Airport (DCA)

        • Take the George Washington Parkway North.
        • Take Route 50 West Exit towards Arlington, Virginia.
        • After entering onto Route 50, immediately cross three lanes and get into the right hand lane.
        • Take the Rosslyn/Key Bridge Exit.
        • Turn right off exit onto N. Lynn Street.
        • Immediately get into the far left lane and merge onto the left lane ramp.
        • Make a left on Wilson Boulevard.
        • Make a left on N. Nash Street.
        • N. Nash will dead-end into N. 17th Street.
        • Building is straight ahead.
        • Parking: Bear left; turn right into parking garage.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.

        From Virginia via Route 50

        • Take Route 50 East toward Washington, D.C.
        • Just past the Ft. Myer military base, get in the left hand lane. Take the left exit for Courthouse Road.
        • At the yield sign, bear left up the hill.
        • At the fourth traffic light Courthouse Road ends into Clarendon Blvd. Turn right on Clarendon.
        • After approximately 1/2 mile, the road will fork.
        • Bear to the right for local traffic, which will put you on N. 17th Street.
        • NEMA will be on the right.
        • The parking garage is just past the office building on the right.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.

        From Washington Dulles National Airport (IAD)

        • Take the Dulles Access Road toward Washington.
        • Stay on Dulles Access Road all the way to Route 66 East.
        • Take 66 East to the Rosslyn/Key Bridge Exit.
        • At the second light (N. Ft. Myer) turn right (WestPark Hotel).
        • At first stop light turn right (this will turn into N. Nash Street).
        • Follow N. Nash Street across Wilson Blvd. (1 stop light).
        • N. Nash Street will dead-end at N. 17th Street.
        • Building is straight ahead.
        • Parking: Bear left; turn right into parking garage.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.

        PLEASE NOTE: Route 66 East is HOV-2 from 5:30 to 9:30 a.m. Route 50 is an alternate route.

        From Virginia via I- 395

        • Take 395 towards Washington.
        • Take Rosslyn/Crystal City exit.
        • Follow signs towards Rosslyn.
        • Signs will lead you to Route 50.
        • Take Rosslyn/Key Bridge exit off Route 50.
        • Take your second left onto Wilson Boulevard.
        • Take a left on N. Nash Street (you will see Hyatt on right).
        • N. Nash will dead-end into N. 17th Street. Building is straight ahead.
        • Parking: Bear left; turn right into parking garage.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.
        		From Baltimore-Washington International Airport (BWI)
        • Exit from car rental lot headed for Baltimore/Washington 295.
        • Take second right exit, 2B-295 (South) BW Parkway/Washington.
        • Bear right after MD 202 Exit. Note on your left the Prince George's Hospital Center on hill. 23 miles from BWI.
        • Exit at New York Avenue. right lane. 24 miles from BWI Richmond.
        • Continue on New York Avenue.
        • Get into your left lane marked. 395 Tunnel South.
        • Continue thru 2nd Tunnel. Immediately get in the right lane to take Richmond 395 South. 30 miles from BWI.
        • Bear left coming onto the four lanes marked 395 South/Route 1 South.
        • Keep right 395 South.
        • Exit right at 9A Rosslyn. 33 miles from BWI.
        • Follow directions on Clark St. directing toward 66 West
          Road forks. Take left fork toward Rosslyn/Wilson Blvd.
        • Do not go right toward Rt. 66.
        • Continue through stop lights for North Lynn and Ft. Myer.
        • Third stop light. turn left at North Nash & 17th Street.
        • Building is straight ahead. Parking: Bear left; turn right into parking garage.
        • Upon arrival, please visit the NEMA Conference Center on the first floor.
